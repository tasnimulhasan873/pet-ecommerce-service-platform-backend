# Puchito Pet Platform - Complete Project Documentation

## Executive Summary

**Puchito** is a comprehensive digital platform designed to revolutionize pet care by combining online shopping for pet products with professional veterinary consultation services. The platform serves as a one-stop solution for pet owners, connecting them with quality pet supplies and certified veterinarians through a seamless online experience.

---

## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Project Name

**Puchito Pet Care & E-commerce Platform**

### 1.2 Problem Statement

Pet owners face multiple challenges in caring for their pets:

- **Fragmented Services**: Need to visit different places for pet products and veterinary advice
- **Time Constraints**: Difficulty scheduling vet appointments during business hours
- **Limited Access**: Remote areas lack immediate access to veterinary professionals
- **Product Trust**: Uncertainty about quality and safety of pet products
- **Emergency Consultation**: No quick way to consult with vets for urgent concerns

### 1.3 Solution Provided

Puchito offers an integrated platform where pet owners can:

- Purchase verified, quality pet products with home delivery
- Book video consultations with certified veterinarians at their convenience
- Manage all pet-related activities in one secure account
- Access professional pet care advice and community support

### 1.4 Target Users

1. **Pet Owners** - Primary users who shop and book consultations
2. **Veterinarians** - Licensed professionals providing online consultations
3. **Platform Administrators** - Team members managing operations
4. **Business Managers** - Oversight and analytics access

### 1.5 Business Value

- **For Pet Owners**: Convenience, time savings, better pet care access
- **For Veterinarians**: Flexible work arrangements, expanded client reach
- **For Business**: Scalable revenue model through products and services
- **For Industry**: Modernizes pet care delivery and accessibility

- View consultation fee breakdown

**Step 5: Confirmation**

- Get unique appointment ID
- Receive email with appointment details

- Calendar reminder sent to both parties

**Step 6: Consultation Day**

- Receive reminder notification before appointment
- Click secure video link to join consultation

- Doctor can provide advice, prescriptions, or referrals

**Step 7: Follow-Up**

- Appointment history saved in user account
- Access past consultation records anytime
- Option to rebook with same doctor
- Leave review and rating after consultation

**Safety Features:**

- Profile completion mandatory before booking
- Payment processed before appointment confirmation

## Appendix: Glossary of Terms

**Platform**: The complete system including website, database, and all functionality

**User/Customer**: Pet owner who shops and books consultations

**Doctor/Veterinarian**: Licensed professional providing consultations

**Administrator**: Platform staff managing operations

**Consultation**: Video call appointment between pet owner and veterinarian

**E-commerce**: Online buying and selling of pet products

**Telehealth**: Remote healthcare services via technology

**Payment Gateway**: Secure system processing online payments (Stripe)

**Dashboard**: Control panel showing information and statistics

**Verification**: Process confirming doctor credentials and licenses

**Shopping Cart**: Virtual basket holding items before purchase

**Checkout**: Process of completing purchase and payment

**Order Fulfillment**: Packing and shipping purchased products

**Revenue**: Money earned by the business

**Scalability**: Ability to grow and handle more users

**Cloud Hosting**: Renting server space from professional providers

**API**: Technology allowing different systems to connect

**Encryption**: Scrambling data for security

**Compliance**: Following laws and regulations
